Short Bio

He was born in a small village of the city of Gaoan, Jiangxi province, China, in 1983. After more than five years mixed role as a student, farmer and cowboy, he left for the town to study and live in a middle school. From then on he spent most of the time in schools and universities. He received his B.Eng. degree in Automation and M.Eng. degree in Detecting Technique and Automation Device from Tianjin University, China, in 2005 and 2007, respectively, and his Ph.D. degree in Communication Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2012. With intrinsic interests and curiosity, he has been working towards solving interesting theoretical and practical problems encountered in multiple disciplines.

Research Interests

His research interest is in system control and optimization, with emphasis on theoretical developments motivated by real-world applications. Specific interests include: data/event-based control and optimization, control structure design, control and optimization of networked systems, operations research, with applications to power & energy systems and industrial processes.
